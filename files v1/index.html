为什么是模型数据Why model data

草稿 · 可改draft · editable

过去六年，我一直在做同一件事：把城市、土地、生态这些专业领域，从混乱的多源数据里，整理成结构化、可量化、经得起检验的东西。做得越深，我越清楚——决定一个模型上限的，常常不是算法，而是数据的质量、结构与公平性。这正是我想投身模型数据的原因。我真正的长处不是某一个学科，而是「快速切入一个陌生的专业领域，把它拆解成高质量数据」这件事；它已经在规划、碳、住房、公共健康等多个领域被反复验证。我希望把这项能力，用在离 AGI 更近的地方。

For the past six years I have done one thing: take specialized fields — cities, land, ecosystems — and turn messy, multi-source data into something structured, quantifiable, and rigorous. The deeper I go, the clearer it gets that what caps a model is often not the algorithm but the quality, structure, and fairness of its data. That is why I want to work on model data. My real strength is not any single discipline but the meta-skill of entering an unfamiliar professional field and decomposing it into high-quality data — proven across planning, carbon, housing, and public health. I want to bring that closer to AGI.

精选项目Selected work

住房公平 × 空间因果Housing equity × spatial causality

第一作者First author

构建杭州 2012–2023 年 118,181 条房产交易 + 基础设施空间数据集；用空间计量与因果推断，量化绿/灰基础设施对住房价格与交易活跃度的成本—收益动态。Built a dataset of 118,181 Hangzhou housing transactions + infrastructure geodata (2012–2023); used spatial econometrics and causal inference to quantify the cost-benefit dynamics of green vs. grey infrastructure on prices and turnover.

118,181 records · spatial econometrics · causal inference  →  Habitat International, 2026

碳储量的多尺度分析Multi-scale carbon storage

共同一作Co-first

369 城 × 3 期面板数据；InVEST + 多元回归 + Moran's I 空间自相关，拆解城市发展对碳储量的多尺度、地区分异驱动。A panel of 369 cities across 3 periods; InVEST + multiple regression + Moran's I spatial autocorrelation to decompose how urban development drives carbon storage at multiple scales.

369 cities · panel · spatial autocorrelation  →  Ecological Indicators, 2023 (IF 6.9)

碳不公平格局与配额分配Carbon inequality & quota allocation

地理学报Acta Geographica Sinica

基于市级投入产出表构建碳转移网络；Tapio 脱钩 + 随机森林 + Shapley 值 + 合作博弈，构建兼顾效率与公平的长三角碳配额分解框架。Built a carbon-transfer network from city-level input-output tables; Tapio decoupling + random forest + Shapley value + cooperative game theory to design an efficiency-and-fairness carbon quota framework for the Yangtze River Delta.

input-output · random forest · Shapley value · cooperative game  →  Acta Geographica Sinica, 2026

生态系统服务供需驱动力Ecosystem-service supply–demand drivers

InVEST 量化供需 + 地理探测器–随机森林（OPGD-RF）框架，从单因子、双因子交互到多因子解析供需比的时空演变机制。InVEST for supply–demand + an optimal-parameter geo-detector–random forest (OPGD-RF) framework analyzing single-factor, interaction, and multi-factor drivers of the supply–demand ratio.

InVEST · geo-detector · random forest  →  Environmental Science, 2025
